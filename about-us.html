What is the MTS's Silicon Valley

"The Silicon VR" is an innovative project designed to transform the remote work landscape by seamlessly integrating the cutting-edge ethos of Silicon Valley into a virtual reality (VR) environment. The primary goal is to facilitate more effective interactions among remote workers, fostering collaboration and strengthening professional bonds. This metaverse aims to create a virtual workspace that not only emulates the technological advancements of Silicon Valley but also harnesses its collaborative and entrepreneurial spirit.
