ABOUT US

What is the MTS's Silicon Valley

"The Silicon VR" is an innovative project designed to transform the remote work landscape by seamlessly integrating the cutting-edge ethos of Silicon Valley into a virtual reality (VR) environment. The primary goal is to facilitate more effective interactions among remote workers, fostering collaboration and strengthening professional bonds. This metaverse aims to create a virtual workspace that not only emulates the technological advancements of Silicon Valley but also harnesses its collaborative and entrepreneurial spirit.

What we provide

  • Exclusive Virtual Land Acquisition:

    • Silicon VR offers a meticulously curated selection of virtual land parcels strategically located in the metaverse. Each parcel is chosen for its potential to become a thriving hub for technological advancements and business innovation.

  • Customizable Virtual Environments:

    • We understand that each company has unique branding and operational needs. Our team of virtual architects and designers collaborate with businesses to create personalized and immersive virtual environments that reflect their corporate identity, values, and objectives.

  • Tech-Ready Infrastructure:

    • Silicon VR ensures that all virtual lands come equipped with state-of-the-art, tech-ready infrastructure. This includes high-speed connectivity, advanced security features, and integration capabilities with cutting-edge technologies such as augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and the Internet of Things (IoT).

  • Metaverse Integration Consulting:

    • As pioneers in the metaverse space, we offer consulting services to guide businesses through the seamless integration of their operations into virtual environments. From virtual storefronts to interactive customer experiences, Silicon VR ensures that companies make the most of their metaverse presence.

Rules and guidelines

  • Land Allocation Criteria:

    • Define clear criteria for allocating virtual land to businesses. Consider factors such as industry relevance, innovation potential, and contribution to the overall ecosystem.

  • Zoning Regulations:

    • Implement zoning regulations within the metaverse to designate specific areas for different purposes (e.g., technology, entertainment, education). This ensures a balanced and diversified virtual environment.

  • Building Codes and Aesthetics:

    • Enforce building codes to maintain a visually cohesive and organized metaverse. Establish aesthetic guidelines to prevent virtual clutter and maintain a professional appearance.
